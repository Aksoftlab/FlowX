What is FlowX?

FlowX is a smart field collection and loan recovery application designed to replace traditional paper allocations and repetitive spreadsheet work. It empowers field executives with a complete digital allocation system, enabling faster decisions, accurate customer tracking, and seamless collection management directly from a mobile device.

Why FlowX?

  • Digital customer allocation instead of printed sheets.
  • Instant customer search without opening Excel files repeatedly.
  • One-tap customer calling.
  • Quick feedback recording after every visit.
  • Promise-to-Pay tracking with reminders.
  • Collection performance monitoring in real time.
  • Separate Hard Resolution cases for better follow-up.
  • Access your work anytime, anywhere.

Benefits for Field Executives

FlowX saves time, reduces paperwork, minimizes errors, improves follow-up efficiency, and keeps every customer interaction organized. Everything required during field recovery is available in one professional mobile application.
